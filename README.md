## 🌌 About Me

I am a results-driven **Mechanical Engineer** who bridges the gap between physical product architecture and interactive digital ecosystems. I specialize in high-precision CAD/BIM modeling, advanced engineering simulations, and developing responsive, lightweight web applications.
